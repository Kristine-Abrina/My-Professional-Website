GOSCHED: Optimizing Patient Scheduling Using Genetic Algorithms with Random Forest for Patient Volume Prediction At The Queen's Clinic

Kristine H. Abrina, Russel Ron Y. Dela Cruz, John Patrick S. Semillano, Earl Lanz Cristien M. Tan

Institution: FEU Institute of Technology Date: May 2025 Type: Undergraduate Thesis

Project Overview

GOSCHED is an intelligent scheduling system developed to optimize patient appointment management at The Queen's Clinic through the integration of advanced machine learning algorithms. The system addresses critical challenges in healthcare scheduling, including prolonged patient wait times, inefficient resource allocation, and staff workload imbalances. By leveraging Genetic Algorithms for schedule optimization and Random Forest Algorithms for predictive analytics, GOSCHED automates the creation of efficient appointment schedules while forecasting patient volumes across the clinic's three branches. The system considers multiple constraints including doctor availability, patient preferences, appointment duration, and medical specialties to generate optimal schedules that minimize conflicts and enhance operational efficiency. Survey results based on ISO 25010 standards and the Technology Acceptance Model demonstrated strong system performance with weighted means of 4.06 and 4.32 respectively, indicating high user satisfaction and acceptance. The predictive analytics component achieved exceptional accuracy with R² scores ranging from 0.9529 to 0.9860 across all branches, enabling proactive resource planning and data-driven decision-making for healthcare administrators.

Key Features

  • Genetic Algorithm Optimization: Automated schedule generation using evolutionary algorithms that optimize for doctor availability, patient preferences, and resource constraints
  • Random Forest Prediction: Machine learning-based forecasting of weekly patient appointment volumes with high accuracy (R² > 0.95) across all clinic branches
  • Multi-Branch Support: Comprehensive scheduling system managing appointments across Caloocan, Fairview, and Bulacan clinic locations
  • Predictive Analytics Dashboard: Admin and manager interface displaying forecasted patient volumes and operational insights for proactive resource planning
  • Constraint-Based Scheduling: Intelligent handling of doctor specialties (OBGYN, Pediatrics), working hours, room availability, and appointment durations
  • Patient Preference Management: System considers multiple patient preferences for doctors and time slots to enhance patient satisfaction
  • Real-Time Schedule Management: Web-based interface allowing authorized staff to view, edit, and manage schedules with role-based access control
  • Data Security: Implementation of Fernet encryption for protecting personally identifiable information (PII) in compliance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012
  • No-Show and Cancellation Handling: Automated tracking and management of appointment cancellations and no-shows to optimize schedule efficiency
  • Model Retraining Pipeline: Monthly automated model updates to maintain prediction accuracy with new appointment data
