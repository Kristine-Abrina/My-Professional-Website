One Piyu Product Analytics Dashboard Planned Project

Kristine H. Abrina

Status: In Planning Phase Type: Data Analytics Dashboard

Project Overview

The One Piyu Product Analytics Dashboard is a planned data visualization and analysis tool designed to provide insights into the marketplace dynamics of One Piyu, an exclusive Facebook community for FEU Institute of Technology students and alumni. This vibrant online community is not only a place to vent or ask questions, but also a hub where students actively buy and sell various products ranging from homemade food items and beverages to secondhand clothing, textbooks, gadgets, and other student essentials. The dashboard aims to aggregate and analyze posting data from the group to identify trending product categories, peak selling periods, and engagement patterns. By employing web scraping techniques (with proper permissions) and natural language processing for post categorization, the system will quantify which types of products generate the most listings, attract the highest engagement (likes, comments, shares), and maintain consistent demand throughout academic periods. This analytical tool will serve multiple purposes: helping student sellers understand market demand to optimize their offerings, assisting buyers in discovering trending products and reliable sellers, and providing the FEU community with data-driven insights into student entrepreneurship patterns and economic activity within their exclusive marketplace ecosystem.

Planned Features

  • Product Category Analysis: Automated classification and quantification of advertised products into categories such as food, clothing, electronics, textbooks, and services
  • Engagement Metrics Dashboard: Interactive visualizations displaying likes, comments, shares, and response rates for different product categories to identify high-engagement items
  • Trending Products Tracker: Real-time identification and ranking of the most frequently posted and discussed products within specific time periods
  • Volume Analysis: Comprehensive tracking of posting frequency by product type, revealing which categories have the highest market supply and seller competition
  • Temporal Trend Analysis: Time-series visualizations showing how product popularity and posting frequency change throughout the academic calendar, exam periods, and holidays
  • Price Range Analytics: Statistical analysis of price distributions across product categories, helping identify typical pricing patterns and potential market gaps
  • Seller Activity Metrics: Anonymized data on posting frequency and engagement rates to understand seller behavior patterns without compromising privacy
  • Interactive Data Filters: Customizable dashboard controls allowing users to filter analytics by date range, product category, engagement threshold, and other relevant parameters
  • Comparative Analysis Tools: Side-by-side comparison features for analyzing multiple product categories or time periods simultaneously
  • Export and Reporting: Functionality to generate downloadable reports and visualizations for academic research or business planning purposes
